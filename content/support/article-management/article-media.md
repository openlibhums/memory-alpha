# Videos and other media

Videos can be published on Janeway in one of two ways.

## Large videos

In most cases, it is best to upload the video to a service like Vimeo and then embed that link in the XML or HTML of the published article. Readers of the article page will see the video and be able to play it directly on the page.

## Small videos

As an alternative, small videos (usually less than 4 GB in size) can be hosted directly on Janeway, but the streaming speed may not match users’ expectations. If a journal wishes to use this workflow, they need to ask their server administrator to allow video uploads.
