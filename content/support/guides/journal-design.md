# Journal design

## Clarity theme

The Clarity theme is the recommended base for styling your journal, press, or repository on Janeway. Clarity prioritizes accessibility and easy customization of fonts, images, and colours. Examples of journals on the Clarity theme:
- [Journal of Cultural Analytics](https://culturalanalytics.org/)

The Clean theme is the predecessor to Clarity. Here are some journals using Clean:
- [Energy Humanities](https://www.openlibhums.org/ehums/)
- [European Journal of Psychoanalysis](https://ejp.normandy.janeway.systems/)
- [Free & Equal](https://freeandequaljournal.org/)
- [Philosophical Logic](https://www.philosophical-logic.org/)
- [Political Philosophy](https://politicalphilosophyjournal.org/)

There are also legacy themes--Material and OLH--but these are not recommended for new journals.

Theme colours and fonts can be customized using a few lines of CSS, and we can provide templates and help make simple changes.
